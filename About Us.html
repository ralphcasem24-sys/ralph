About Us

Our story began with a child walking through the streets, selling lumpia and turon with a smile and a dream. At just seven years old, I learned that every roll we sold carried not only flavor but also the effort, love, and hope of our family.

As I grew older and school became my focus, I could no longer help with selling — but I stayed close to what mattered most. I helped prepare the wrappers, cooked the fillings, and learned the value of patience and care in every step.

Today, that same love and dedication live on in everything we make. Each product is a reflection of our story — a story of family, perseverance, and the joy of sharing simple food that brings people together.
